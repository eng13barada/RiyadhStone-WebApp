# RiyadhStone® Image Prompts & Usage

## Global Aesthetic

- **Atmosphere**: Warm, premium, architectural, disciplined.
- **Lighting**: Soft studio or crisp architectural daylight (Golden Hour).
- **Textures**: Honed limestone, sandblasted surfaces, point-cloud dots.

## Core Prompts

1. **Macro Stone**: "macro warm limestone surface, honed finish, soft studio light, ultra realistic, high detail, 8k"
2. **Façade Detail**: "architectural limestone façade detail, modern metal frames, premium daylight, realistic sharp edges"
3. **Engineering Context**: "engineer reviewing stone shop drawings on a tablet, minimal office, warm palette, realistic, shallow depth of field"
4. **Quality Control**: "quality inspection caliper measurement on a large stone slab close-up, premium documentary style, realistic"
5. **Technical Visualization**: "photogrammetry point cloud of a modern building exterior, subtle premium tech visualization, amber and dark ink tones"
6. **Logistics**: "stacked stone sample boards with QR tags, clean organized finishing workshop, realistic lighting"

## Usage Rules

- **Parallax**: Always apply `ParallaxBg` to full-width stone imagery.
- **Glassmorphism**: Overlay `glass-card` for text blocks over complex background images.
- **Noise**: Global SVG noise must be active at 0.05 opacity.
